About Me

Hey there!

I’m Jon De Heus, a user experience designer and researcher who is currently based out of Austin, TX. I have over 7 years of experience working in B2B and B2C companies, and I’ve had experience working in companies that range in size from scrappy startups with under 100 people to Fortune 50 companies with over 100,000 people.

Currently, I’m a product designer at Ford Credit, where I’m responsible for working on Account Manager, which is how millions of Ford Credits’ lease and retail customers pay their monthly bills and check up on their payment status.

Want to get in touch?

If you’d like to get in touch with me about a job opportunity, or just want to connect so we can talk about UX, feel free to either email me or connect on LinkedIn.
